White Foxes


“White Foxes,
it’s a love song, and it’s,
basically about wanting to be,
peaceful in a way,

very peaceful.

And to me, the most idyllic
place to cleanse yourself is
in the middle of nowhere

The only thing you can see
is snow and white foxes

I just think it’s a nice image.”



- Susanne Sundfør , 2012
