Lil B - I'm God

Brandon Christopher McCartney  (born August 17, 1989), professionally known as Lil B and as his alter ego The BasedGod, is an American rapper.  His solo work spans several genres, including hip hop, new age, indie rock and choral music. He describes his work as "based", a term which denotes a lifestyle of positivity and tolerance.
