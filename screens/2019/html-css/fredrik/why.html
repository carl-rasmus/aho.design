This is a Norwegian saying based around the city of Drammen, south-west of Oslo. The saying goes (directly translated) "It´s better with a schnaps an hour, then an hour in Drammen. In Norwegian, a "Dram" is a small shot or schnaps of liqour. Some of the other answers are based on Åge Alexandersens song "Fire Pils og en Pizza" (Four beers, and one pizza)
